My Favorite Animal

The Blue Morpho Butterfly


The morpho butterflies comprise many species of Neotropical butterfly under the genus Morpho. This genus includes over 29 accepted species and 147 accepted subspecies, found mostly in South America, Mexico, and Central America.
The blue morpho is among the largest butterflies in the world, with wings spanning from five to eight inches.
Their vivid, iridescent blue coloring is a result of the microscopic scales on the backs of their wings, which reflect light.

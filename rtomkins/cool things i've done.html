Gone on FUGE trips

Every summer CIBC, Cayman Islands Baptist Church, takes a trip to a FUGE camp somewhere in the US. FUGE is a church camp where you and your youth group attend for a week. There are numberous activities to choose from and you have your own personalized schedule for the week. You get to meet new people and strengthen our relationship with God. In the past years I've been to South Carolina, North Carolina, and Louisana.
